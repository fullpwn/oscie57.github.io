Using DVD-Rs (Burned Games) on a Wii

I am quite poor to be honest, but I really want to enjoy Wii games. I recently found a big stack of DVD-R discs from a while ago, and I wondered; "Can I burn Wii/GameCube/Wii U games to these?". For Wii U, no, you cannot. For GameCube, while it is possible, it is extremely annoying so I wont go into it. But, Wii, on the other hand, isn't too difficult, as long as you know what you are doing. For burning the actual games to a disc, see "Burning Wii Games to a DVD-R (Coming Soon)".

      0. Your Wii MUST be modded beforehand, with Priiloader installed. Your Wii must also be able to read DVD-R discs. Chances are it does, but I am not the one to confirm it.
      1. Open up Priiloader by holding "reset" upon boot.
      2. Make your way to "System Menu Hacks" and press "Up" twice on the Wii Remote to get to "Force Disc Games to run under IOS249"
        Note: if this IOS is a stub, then whoops you've got an issue. Make sure to have cIOS installed.
      3. Press "Save Settings" and then press B to get to the main menu.
      4. Go down to "Settings" and make your way down to "Use System Menu IOS" and switch it to "off".
      5. Below this setting, you should now see "IOS to use for SM". Press the left button on the DPAD until you see "249".
      6. Once you have done this, make sure to press "Save Settings" and press B to go to the main menu.
      7. Go to "System Menu" and press A. You should return to the Home Menu.

This is how I got DVD-Rs to work on my Wii, so I hope it works for you too! Everything I do is done on 4.3E so results may vary depending on region. Hopefully, if you've done it right (and your Wii supports DVD-Rs), your disc will load in the Disc Channel.
