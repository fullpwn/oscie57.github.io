Running Korean discs on a European Wii

I have been learning Korean recently (specifically Hangul at the moment) and I thought, what if I played some Wii games in Korean to get used to the language, so I burned some games to DVD-Rs and played them. Some games however, dont really work. I had Rhythm Heaven Fever (리듬 세상 Wii) burned and at hand so I tried it and it didn't work at first, so go here for guidance on getting DVD-Rs to run on your Wii.

      0. If you are using a burned Disc, your Wii must be DVD-R compatible!
      1. Make sure you have setup WiiFlow on your Wii.
      2. Insert the Disc in your Wii normally, DO NOT open it through the normal Disc Channel.
      3. Open up WiiFlow through however you have set it up, either through a forwarder, or in the Homebrew Channel.
      4. Move your Wii "Cursor" to the bottom of the screen, you should see a Disc icon. Press on it and the game should load.

That is what I did and it worked fine for me, though your results may vary. Currently, I havent been able to get Wii Play: Motion (Wii 리모컨플러스로 즐기는 버라이어티 게임 박스) working, but other games such as New Super Mario Bros. Wii (뉴 수파 마리오브라더스 Wii) and Wii Sports work fine with this method.
